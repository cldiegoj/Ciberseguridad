## Background 

- ISO is the "Internal Organization for Standardization"
- Established in 1947 to develop international standars for quality, safety, efficiency, interoperability and consistency across industries
- Recognized worldwide by corporations, governments and consumers
- Conformity to ISO standars is often a baseline requirement for organizations or governments producing/procuring goods and services
- ISO 9001 is widely used for demostrating product/service quality and customer satisfaction
